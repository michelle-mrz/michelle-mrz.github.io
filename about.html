Hi, this is Michelle!

I’m a Computer Science student at Cornell University from Iquique, Chile currently based in upstate New York. My interests lie in the intersections of design and computing. At Cornell, I serve as the Design Lead of BigRed//Hacks, the largest student-run hackathon on campus. Currently, I’m a Research Assistant at Cornell’s Digital Fashion and Body Scan Research Lab under the supervision of Dr. Fatma Baytar, working mainly on VR development and 3D data analysis.

In my free time, I enjoy making art, learning new languages, watching anime and Game of Thrones, online shopping, browsing the Kardashian subreddit, and spending quality time with family and friends.
